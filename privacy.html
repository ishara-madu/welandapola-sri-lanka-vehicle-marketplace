Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Display Name: To identify you to other users on the platform.
  • Email Address: Used for secure login, registration, and password recovery.
  • Phone Number: Used to allow buyers to contact you when you list a vehicle.
  • Vehicle Details & Images: Public listings that you publish, hosted securely on Cloudinary.

Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. All user data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS, and authentication credentials are managed securely through Firebase Authentication.

Data Deletion Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete your individual vehicle listings directly within the app at any time. If you wish to delete your entire account and all associated personal data from our servers, you can request account deletion by sending a message to isharamadushankab@gmail.com.
